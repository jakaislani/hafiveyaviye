How can I talk to Nickelodeon?

Contacting Nickelodeon Via Phone. Use 1-212-846-2543 for the corporate offices in New York. Calling Nickelodeon directly isn’t a matter of using one single telephone number — the network has regional offices all over the world, each with its own number.

How do you pitch to Nickelodeon?

If you have a programming idea, you must have a licensed agent or attorney contact Nickelodeon Development in writing at Nickelodeon 1515 Broadway, New York, New York 10036 to request a “Submission Release” form. The proposal must be accompanied by the signed submission release for review.

How do I submit an idea to Nickelodeon?

Submissions must be uploaded by midnight (PST) on AUGUST 1st. All submission materials should be completed and uploaded to the Nickelodeon online submission platform via Coverfly. Once in the platform, fill in the form with your “Project Info” about your spec from the Accepted Shows List.

How much does the Nickelodeon Writing Program Pay?

Writer salaries at Nickelodeon can range from $50,418-$65,153.
