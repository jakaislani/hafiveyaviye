John Laurinaitis’ wife is Kathy Colace Laurinaitis.

The pair got engaged on September 3, 2015.

Kathy Colace Laurinaitis and John Laurinaitis married in a private ceremony on March 24, 2016, and have since been together.

Kathy Laurinaitis is an American businesswoman.

She was born on  October 19, 1964, in Imperial Valley in southern California.

Kathy’s and her parents moved to Phoenix when she was a teenager but remained in Phoenix, making it her home when her parents returned to California several years later.

She established JBN & Associates in 1999, with each initial coming from her own children’s names after working as a land developer. It started as a staffing company only doing a placement in Arizona but has grown into a boutique executive search firm serving North America with a team of six at remote locations in Arizona, California, Wisconsin, and Ohio.

The company recruits in all areas of expertise but is widely known in land development, construction, home building, food and manufacturing, and e-commerce.

Kathy Colace Laurinaitis, the managing partner of JBN & Associates is the recipient of the Brain Injury Alliance of Arizona’s (BIAAZ) Woman of Courage Award.

In June 2020, she underwent surgery to remove a blueberry-sized mass from her brain stem that was initially mistakenly diagnosed as Bell’s Palsy.

In September 2020, a titanium weight was placed in her upper eyelid, and surgeons removed six inches of muscle in her thigh to create a sort of hammock in her lower eyelid to enable blinking.

In February 2021, she had another surgery with titanium weight to help her eyes to blink. She still does acupuncture, as well as pulsed electromagnetic field therapy (PEMF).

Kathy Colace has three children with ex-husband Jon Garcia; Nikki Bella, Brie Bella and JJ Garcia. She also has a stepdaughter; Maya Laurinaitis.
