Keeping Up with the Kardashians: Who is Malika Haqqs boyfriend in 2020? Sun 9 February 2020

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Keeping Up With The Kardashians star Malika Haqq is known to many as the ultimate Kardashian bestie.

She’s been with Khloe through thick and thin and is pretty much baby True’s second mum.

However, the reality TV star does enjoy a life outside of the famous family and has dated a string of famous faces – that you may not have expected –  over the years.

So, who is Malika Haqq’s boyfriend in 2020? Here are all the details you need to know.

Who is Malika Haqq’s boyfriend in 2020?

Malika has almost always kept details of her current relationship status quiet.

She announced her pregnancy in September 2019 via Instagram. It’s been confirmed in 2020 that the father of Malika’s baby is rapper O.T. Genasis. So, from this, we can assume that the couple is still dating.

Malika shared an Instagram snap of the couple in December 2018.

But in September 2019, O.T Genasis was stopped for an interview by Hip Hollywood where he managed to dodge answering any questions about him and Malika.
