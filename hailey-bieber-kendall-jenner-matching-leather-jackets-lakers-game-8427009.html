Hailey Bieber and Kendall Matched in Leather Jackets at the Lakers Game

July 2024 · 2 minute read

You may choose to showcase your friendship via Taylor Swift-inspired friendship bracelets, but Hailey Bieber and Kendall Jenner are making a case for swapping that tiny trinket for something a little more statement-making: coordinating leather coats. Last night, the two besties took in the Lakers vs. Oklahoma City Thunder game at Crypto.com Arena in L.A. and kicked back in their courtside seats in matching long leather coats and showed fans exactly how to look cool and laid-back when you're sitting pretty for everyone in the arena and all the viewers at home.

The two were joined by friends Sarah Staudinger and Lauren Perez. Jenner wore an all-leather outfit that brought together a long black coat and matching pants. She added heeled boots to finish off the look. Not one to let an opportunity for self-promotion go to waste, she sipped from a bedazzled cup 818 cup. For Bieber's part, she kept things low-key, wearing a backward gray baseball cap and her long, black leather coat.

The basketball game is just the latest BFF outing from Bieber and Jenner. The two spent the New Year's holiday together on a tropical vacation. They were joined by Hailey's husband, Justin, and — as eagle-eyed fans noticed — Jenner's ex (who might not be an ex, if rumors are to be believed) Bad Bunny. Jenner doesn't just love a matching bestie moment. When she and Bunny, whose real name is Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio, were together, they also had a matching moment.
