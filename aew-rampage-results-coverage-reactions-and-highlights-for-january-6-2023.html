AEW Rampage Results Coverage, Reactions and Highlights For January 6, 2023

June 2024 · 5 minute read

It’s Friday night, and you know what that means, it’s time for AEW Rampage and Ringside News has you covered with live results.

Start time for Rampage is scheduled for 10:00 PM EST Match-by-match highlights and results from the show will take place on this very page. Feel free to leave any reactions in the comment section below.

Tonight’s Rampage is set to feature the following matches:

Darby Allin vs Mike Bennett

Jon Moxley & Bryan Danielson vs Top Flight

Jamie Hayter & Dr. Britt Baker D.M.D. vs Robyn & Charlette Renegade

Preston Vance vs an unknown opponent

It should be interesting to see how things play out on tonight’s show.

Jon Moxley & Bryan Danielson vs Top Flight

Rampage kicks off with Top Flight taking out Moxley during his entrance, Danielson responds by hitting a double Tope Suicida. The match officially begins with Darius Martin and Moxley in the ring. Darius hits a vertical suplex and Dante tags in and hits a splash over the top rope to Moxley. Moxley counters a sunset flip and tags Danielson in who hits a series of kicks to Dante on the mat. Danielson hits a running drop kick in the corner. Danielson hits a huricanrana but Dante flips through to his feet and tags Darius in who hits a drop kick. Moxley tags in as Dante tags back in and springboard dives off the top rope but Moxley counters with a kick. All four wrestlers get in the ring and start brawling before the brawl spills outside the ring. Back in the ring Danielson gets Dante into the Romero special before clawing at Dante’s face and transitioning int the dragon sleeper. Danielson tags Moxley back in who hits a kick to the chest. Moxley hits a superplex and then locks in the juji gatame.

*Commercial break*

Back live with Moxley and Danielson hitting double kicks to Dante’s chest. Dante dodges a kick causing Moxley and Danielson’s legs to collide and allowing Darius to tag in. Darius is kicked in the chest by Danielson, Darius catches another kick and hits a standing Spanish fly. Moxley tags in as Dante tags in and hits a top rope dive to Moxley. Dante goes up top and hits a beautiful frog splash but Moxley immediately rolls through to hits a series of elbow strikes. Moxley hits a big lariat and covers for a two count. Danielson tags in as Darius tags in. Danielson hits the running knee and covers but Dante runs in to break it up. Danielson stomps Darius’ head and locks in a sleeper on a knocked out Darius and the ref calls for the bell.

Winners: Jon Moxley & Bryan Danielson

Jamie Hayter & Dr. Britt Baker D.M.D. vs Robyn & Charlette Renegade

Before the bell can even ring Baker and Hayter attack the renegades. Hayter hits a running elbow to Robyn. Robyn hits a drop kick and then running knees in the corner. Charlette tags in and the Renegades hit a double chop to Hayter in the corner. Robyn tags back in and the Renegades go for a double suplex but Hayter counters and suplexes both Renegades to a huge pop from the crowd. Hayter tags Baker in and Baker hits Robyn with a sling blade.

*Commercial break*

Back live with Hayter going for a running splash in the corner but Robyn counters with a big boot. Charlette and Baker both tag in and Charlette hits with a spine buster. Charlette hits a fishermen’s suplex and gets a two count. Baker and Hayter hit a double elbow strike to Charlette and Baker hits the stomp and Baker covers for three.

Winners: Jamie Hayter & Dr. Britt Baker D.M.D.

Preston Vance vs Sonico

The match begins with Sonico hitting a series of strikes that Vance completely no sells and then hits a spine buster. Vance hits a discus lariat and covers for three.

Winner: Preston Vance

After the bell, Vance rips the mask off sonico as the Portland crowd boos incredibly loudly.

*Commercial break*

TNT Championship: Darby Allin vs Mike Bennett

Our main event begins with Allin rising from the corner and walking up to Bennett slowly. Bennett shoves Allin and after exchanging holds Allin hits a side headlock take down. Bennett kicks Allin’s knee and Allin rolls outside and Bennett throws Allin to the barricade. Bennett hits a hard right hand and throws Allin back in the ring. Allin hits a drop kick and goes for a Tope Suicida but Matt Taven steps in to take the hit.

*Commercial break*

Back live with Bennett going for a powerbomb but Allin counters and slams Bennett over his back. Allin rolls out of the ring and puts Bennett on a chair outside. Allin goes up top and hits a drop kick from the top rope to Bennett on the outside floor. Back in the ring Allin goes up top for a coffin drop but Maria gets in the ring and lays over Bennett to protect Bennett. Taven causes a distraction that allows Bennett to slam Allin and cover for a two count. Bennett goes for a piledriver from the top rope but Allin counters with an avalanche code red. Allin goes back up and hits the coffin drop and covers for three.

Winner: Darby Allin

That’s it for this week’s Rampage. Let us know what you thought and we’ll see you back here next Friday for more Rampage coverage. Until then, stay safe!
