Gwen Stefani has been lauded for her vocal prowess throughout her career, and her buttery voice has attracted a massive fanbase. But it seems the iconic singer isn't the only one in her family born with the gift to stun audiences with her singing, as her son, Kingston Rossdale, recently proved.

Stefani shared a video on her TikTok account, which showed her son performing live in front of a small audience in her husband Blake Shelton's Tishomingo-based bar, Ole Red. Stefani shares Kingston with her previous husband, Bush frontman Gavin Rossdale, along with her other sons.

The video clip showed Stefani's 17-year-old son confidently getting up on stage, chatting to the audience with ample self-assurance and eventually starting his set. His mother captioned the video proudly, "Per y'all's request: The full video of Kingston Rossdale PERFORMING at Blake Shelton's bar & grill Ole Red in Tishomingo, OK!"

One fan wrote, "Aw the look of Pride on Blake's face... holding back the tears."

Blake Shelton's Relationship with His Stepson

If Kingston's awe-inspiring performance wasn't enough to get fans clamoring on social media, the boy's stepdad's reaction to his performance surely elicited more than enough buzz. Finishing his set to roaring applause, Kingston was just about ready to leave the stage when Shelton approached.

The country icon had posted that a new artist would be making their debut at his bar on Friday, August 11, already showing massive support for the young Kingston. He made it clear how proud he was of his stepson when he stepped onto the stage to congratulate the blossoming artist on his spectacular performance.
